How the Tour Works

The Midnight Tour focuses on the late-night atmosphere starting at 8:30 PM, specifically targeting Daikoku PA.

The Route:
Hotel Pick-up → A-pit Autobacs → Tokyo Highway Cruise → Daikoku PA

*If Daikoku is Closed:
Police frequently close Daikoku PA late at night on weekends. If we cannot enter Daikoku, the tour will automatically reroute to Umihotaru PA, a massive artificial island in Tokyo Bay known for its own underground late-night car meets.

→ Rainbow Bridge → Hotel Drop-off

  • Runs on Friday and Saturday nights only.
  • Departs around 8:30 PM.
  • Duration: 3.5 – 4 hours.
  • Ride in the legendary Nissan Skyline R34 GT-R (other options available).
